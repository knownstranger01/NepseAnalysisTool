Technical Indicators

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

RSI is a momentum indicator that measures the speed and change of price movements. It ranges from 0 to 100.

  • RSI < 30: Oversold condition, Buy recommendation
  • RSI > 70: Overbought condition, Sell recommendation
  • 30 < RSI < 70: Neutral condition

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

MACD is a trend-following momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two moving averages.

  • MACD > 0: Bullish trend, Buy recommendation
  • MACD < 0: Bearish trend, Sell recommendation

Moving Average

Moving Average is the average price over a specific period. It helps identify the direction of the price trend.

  • Price > MA: Bullish trend, Buy recommendation
  • Price < MA: Bearish trend, Sell recommendation
