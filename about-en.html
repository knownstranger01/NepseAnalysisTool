About Us

NEPSE Tracker is a comprehensive analysis tool for Nepal's stock market. We provide real-time tracking of the NEPSE index and individual stocks, technical analysis, and recommendations.

Our goal is to help investors make informed decisions. We do this by using the latest market data and advanced technical analysis tools.

Our Mission

To provide simple and effective tools to help Nepali investors make informed decisions in the stock market.

Our Vision

To become the leading and reliable analysis platform for investors in Nepal's stock market.
